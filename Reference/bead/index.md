# bead

> The functions prefixed with Mbead make up the Bead module. The Bead module allows you to measure and verify beads, such as strips of glue or lead. For example, you can verify that a bead of glue occurs at a required position, that its width falls within a certain range, that it does not have any missing sections (gaps), and that its gaps do not exceed a specified size.
