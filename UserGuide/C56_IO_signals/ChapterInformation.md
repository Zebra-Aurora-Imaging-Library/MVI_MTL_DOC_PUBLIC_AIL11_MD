# I/O signals and communicating with external devices

This chapter explains how to control and inquire non-video input and output (I/O) signals of your hardware. Note that not all Zebra products have I/O signals. For more information on I/O signals supported by your hardware, refer to the _Aurora Imaging Library Hardware-specific Notes_ for your Zebra product.
