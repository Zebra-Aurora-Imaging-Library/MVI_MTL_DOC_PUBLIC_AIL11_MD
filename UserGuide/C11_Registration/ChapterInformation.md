# Registration

This chapter explains how to use the Aurora Imaging Library Registration module to perform depth-from-focus registration, extended depth of field registration, and photometric stereo registration. The Aurora Imaging Library Registration module can also perform correlation-stitching registration and mosaicing, refer to [Correlation stitching registration and mosaicing](../C12_Correlation_stitching_registration/ChapterInformation.md) .
