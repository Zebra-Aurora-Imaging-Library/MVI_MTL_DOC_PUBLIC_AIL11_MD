# Help overview and customization

Hello, and welcome to what can be described as _Aurora Imaging Library Help for Aurora Imaging Library Help_. Even the Help can use a little help, sometimes.

Aurora Imaging Library Help documents what is available for a major release of Aurora Imaging Library, such as Aurora Imaging Library 11.

The Aurora Imaging Library release notes, accessible from Aurora Imaging Control Center and/or the Zebra Aurora Imaging Library product support webpages (for example, [zebra.com/ail-info](https://zebra.com/ail-info)), can provide additional documentation, such as new or modified features, systems, and products.

## Settings

There are several settings that you can modify in the Help to enhance your experience. To view or change these settings, click on the *[Image: Gear_Button_New_Help.png]*

 button, which you should see at the top-right of almost every page in the Help.

You are absolutely free to modify these settings to find what works best for you. To further enhance your Help experience, see the [Help tips page](S03_Help_tips_online_and_Linux.md). Here you will find guidance on navigation, searching for topics, and collapsing/filtering displayed information.

Some releases of Aurora Imaging Library might be referred to using decimals or roman numerals; for example, Aurora Imaging Library X and Aurora Imaging Library 10. In such cases, 10 and X can be used interchangeably.
