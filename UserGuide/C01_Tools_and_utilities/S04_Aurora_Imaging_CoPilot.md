# Aurora Imaging CoPilot

Aurora Imaging CoPilot, which is accessible from Aurora Imaging Control Center, is your all-encompassing companion GUI for the library. It lets you visually prototype and test your Aurora Imaging Library application and can automatically generate the corresponding code in a variety of languages. This unique and innovative feature essentially gives you the ability to produce Aurora Imaging Library code from a visual environment.

*[Image: sc_CoPilot.png]*

Aurora Imaging CoPilot gives you access to several useful features and utilities, which allow you to acquire/extract information about images, take and display measurements, generate a histogram, and even compare images. Aurora Imaging CoPilot is also particularly adept at helping you train and perform deep learning/machine learning tasks, and to perform accompanying operations, such as data labeling and dataset management.

To stay up to date with the latest features, keep on the look out for [Aurora Imaging CoPilot updates](../../ReleaseNotes/index.md).
