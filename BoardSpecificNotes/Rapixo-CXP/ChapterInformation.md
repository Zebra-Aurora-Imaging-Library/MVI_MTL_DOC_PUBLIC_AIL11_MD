# Zebra Rapixo CXP

This section discusses features of Aurora Imaging Library that are particular to Zebra Rapixo CXP family of boards and ways that optimize their performance. Zebra Rapixo CXP family specific information regarding specific functions is integrated into the functions' reference topics.
