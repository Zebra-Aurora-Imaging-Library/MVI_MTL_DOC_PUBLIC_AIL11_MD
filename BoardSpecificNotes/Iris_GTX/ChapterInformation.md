# Zebra Iris GTX

This section discusses features of Aurora Imaging Library that are particular to the Zebra Iris GTX family of smart cameras and ways that optimize their performance. Zebra Iris GTX information regarding specific functions is integrated into the functions' reference topics. The Aurora Imaging Library release notes, accessible from Aurora Imaging Control Center and/or the Zebra Aurora Imaging Library product support webpages (for example, [zebra.com/ail-info](https://zebra.com/ail-info)), can provide additional documentation, such as new or modified features, systems, and products.
